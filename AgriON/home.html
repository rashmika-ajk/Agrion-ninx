NixORG is a platform that connects farmers directly with customers. Here, people can buy organic food straight from the farm. There's no need to buy food filled with chemicals from the market. With NixORG, you get fresh, natural produce while supporting local farmers.

NixFO is an online forum provided by AgriON where people can share their doubts, solutions, and thoughts about farming. It's a place for farmers and enthusiasts to connect, ask questions, and offer advice. With NixFO, you can learn from others' experiences and contribute your own knowledge to the community.

The Agro Trips section of AgriON connects educational institutions with farms, allowing students to visit agricultural lands and help farmers directly. This hands-on experience is a practical way for students to learn and gain valuable insights into farming. Through Agro Trips, students can enhance their understanding of agriculture by engaging in real-world farming activities.
