About Agro Trips

Students visit farms through Agro Trips to experience agriculture firsthand, gaining practical knowledge that complements their classroom learning. During these visits, experienced farmers share valuable insights and answer questions, enriching students' understanding of farming practices. Additionally, these experiences build meaningful connections between students and local farmers, fostering a deeper appreciation for agriculture and its role in the community.
